Tale A Snap

Zeal Society organised an online event “Tale a snap” in which students had to express their emotions through a story. They also had to upload a picture related to the story. It helped students to expand their horizons of imagination and put words to visuals. This novel kind of event had great participation where students put their creativity and storytelling skills to display . The journey of the stories was tracked through social media via the Instagram page of Zeal Society . The judgement was partially based on the social media engagement of the story and partially on how well the story had been articulated.
