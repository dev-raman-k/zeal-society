Handle A Scandal

“Handle A Scandal “ was held as an interactive and fun ice breaking session to inculcate the social skills and confidence among the students in a creative and interesting manner that would prompt them to express themselves and also to provide some relief to the students from the monotonous academic life.Students were made to discuss any past scandals or controversies that they have been involved in. The judgement was based on fluency, delivery of thoughts and creativity. All in all it was an engaging and interactive event for the incoming freshmen students to get to know about zeal society and its members
